Court Outcome Predictor

This tool analyzes a legal case summary and predicts whether the final decision is likely to be affirmed or reversed, and returns a confidence score.

The model is trained on Supreme-court and lower-court decisions across jurisdictions and is intended to assist researchers and court staff — it does not provide legal advice.

What This System Does

  • Reads and processes your legal case summary
  • Uses a trained machine learning model
  • Predicts if the outcome is affirmed or reversed
  • Shows a confidence level (%)

What You Should Enter

  • Facts of the case
  • Parties involved
  • Key legal issue
  • Lower court decision

Color Meaning Guide

  • Green = High confidence (70–100%)
  • Yellow = Medium confidence (40–69%)
  • Red = Low confidence (0–39%)
